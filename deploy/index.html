Sample Report

What a Full Audit Looks Like

Example based on a wireless earbuds product. Your report follows the same structure.

1. Buyer Pain Points

Pain Point

Battery life too short for daily use

Evidence from Reviews

"Drained in under 2 hours even with ANC off. Had to charge twice during my commute."

Opportunity Note

Lead with total playback hours in title. Buyers research this first.

Pain Point

Poor fit — falls out during exercise

Evidence from Reviews

"Fell out twice during my morning run. Tried all three ear tip sizes."

Opportunity Note

Highlight ear hook design and secure-fit tip options in bullets.

2. Listing Title Ideas

SEO-Focused

Wireless Earbuds with 40-Hour Battery — Active Noise Cancelling, Secure Sport Fit, USB-C Charging

Benefit-Driven

Never Run Out of Music — 40H Battery Earbuds with ANC, All-Day Comfort Fit & Fast Charge

3. Bullet Point Ideas

40-HOUR BATTERY — Built for long commutes, full workdays, and gym sessions without stopping to recharge. 10-minute fast charge adds 2 hours of playback.

STAY-PUT SPORT FIT — Ergonomic ear hooks and 3 sizes of silicone tips keep buds secure during runs, workouts, and commutes. Tested for 2+ hours of active use.

4. Ad Angles

Angle: Battery Anxiety

Hook: Tired of your earbuds dying before your commute ends?

Body: Most earbuds advertise 6 hours — ours actually deliver 40. No stopping to charge. No interruptions.

CTA: Shop Now — 40-Hour Battery Earbuds

Angle: Sport Fit

Hook: Your earbuds shouldn't fall out mid-rep.

Body: Secure sport hooks and 3-size fit tips keep these in place through your hardest workouts.

CTA: Get Your Pair — Free Returns

5. Customer Reply Template

For: "Battery drained too fast"

Hi [Name], thank you for the feedback — we're sorry the battery didn't last as long as you expected. Our earbuds are rated for 8 hours per charge (40 hours total with the case), but ANC and high volume can reduce this. We'd love to help you get the most out of them — please reach out to [email] and we'll make it right.
